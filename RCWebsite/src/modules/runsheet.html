Runsheet Module

A New Workflow

In years of experience we seen lots of ways how to handle your crucial run specific data. Quite often you see the use of Excel sheets. Every team or engineer uses his own design and variables.

This approach was always quite unpractical. It is hard to keep all your files organized, transfer the data or change the design. Especially sharing your runsheets was impossible.

That's why we created a new one that stores the data into a database, gives you a modern interface and solves above problems.

But to make it useful for any team, we made it templatable and customizable. You can create your own design and variables with the template editor.

Full Flexibility
Through Templating

The template editor comes with the Runsheet module. Here you can create your own design in an simple drag and drop interface. You create your own fields here and link them to any variables that exist on the RC Platform.
