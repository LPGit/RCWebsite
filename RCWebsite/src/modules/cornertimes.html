CornerTimes Module

HOW IT WORKS

Corner Times is a real-time easy-to-use lap analysis tool. In a three step procedure it shows you exactly in which corners you lose and gain time compared to other drivers or yourself.

Everytime new data is available, the data is converted to the RC data format and stored on the RC Platform.

The data is then processed. Laps are aligned and split into corners. Delta times are calculated between all available corners. Theoretical best lap times are calculated.

The track can be split into corners automatically by g-forces and damper alignment or you can use the track editor to split the track into predefined segments based on distance.

The result is a real-time view that refreshes every time new data is available. From here it is easy to depict in which corners you are fast and in which corners you need to improve. The first line compares your driver to other drivers and the second line to his last lap.

Direct Plot Access

Comparison of the data of a specific corner can be viewed easily via click on the corresponding corner in the view. A plot window with all the relevant channels will show up immediately.

Direct Video Access

Our video analysis software allows us to detect who is in the video stream picture as well as where and at what time he is visible. Accordingly you can get a video of how a corner was driven by a simple click.

Track Editor

If you want to use a custom segmentation of the track, this can be easily achieved with our custom track editor. Otherwise you can always stick to our highly sophisticated auto solution which splits the track by distance, g-forces and damper alignment.
