RC Platform

In modern motorsports data efficiency is rapidly becoming the most decisive factor for success. The amount of data produced is growing day by day. Therefore engineers need a modern software solution to handle the large amount of data.

To solve the problem as a whole we built a fast, flexible and easy to use platform. It follows a simple but effective process: Import data, process data and evaluate data.

RC Platforms modular design allows the adaption to any enviroment. It works in small scale just as well as in large scale. You can just use a single module as well as many.

RC Modules

CornerTimes

Compare your drivers performance lap by lap, corner by corner in realtime. CornerTimes automatically imports your verhicle data on the fly and does a complete analysis of the performance in each corner. Finding out where you are losing and gaining time has never been this fast and easy.

Runsheet

RC Custom Development
