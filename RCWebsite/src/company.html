Company

Who We Are

Baumgärtner Ltd. Offers racecar engineering, data aquisition and specifically software solutions for the motor racing industry.

Company History

Working in top level motor racing for many years it became more and more clear that the administrative workload of the race engineer had to be reduced so that he could concentrate on the vital job of achieving faster laptimes. Hence the idea was born to develop a motor racing bespoke software for data management. Therefore in 2012 the company Baumgärtner Ltd. was founded doing work in top level racecar engineering and professional software development (engineering). Together with a major manufacturer a online runsheet database was developed and used in DTM. Further development resulted in the RC motorsport platform which is now available in modular form to be used in any category of motorsport. The direct link to racing and ontrack work resulted in a very practical software solution for the racing envoirment.
